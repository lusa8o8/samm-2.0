# samm Brand Spec

## Overview
`samm` is a software brand built around orchestration rather than spectacle. It begins in marketing operations, but the system is designed to extend into broader business workflows where multiple agents, human approvals, and coordinated execution matter.

At its core, `samm` is the coordinating intelligence behind the system. It routes work, keeps specialist agents aligned, and preserves human oversight. The brand should feel calm, capable, and slightly irregular in a way that signals authorship rather than machine polish.

## Brand Idea
- `samm` is the coordinator
- specialist agents execute under its supervision
- humans remain in control of approvals and judgment
- the software should feel adaptive, quiet, and trustworthy

## Positioning
`samm` is an orchestration layer for multi-agent work. It is not positioned as an all-knowing AI assistant or a replacement for people. It is the system that keeps agents, workflows, and decisions aligned.

Primary framing:
- one coordinating system
- many specialist agents
- human oversight where it matters
- clear operational visibility

## Name
Brand name:
- `samm`

Internal meaning:
- loosely derived from `marketing multi-agent system`

Usage rule:
- always lowercase in brand presentation
- do not force the acronym in outward-facing copy

## Logo
Primary logo:
- lowercase `samm` wordmark only

The logo should not rely on a symbol. The wordmark itself is the identity.

Visual character:
- slightly imperfect
- not perfectly geometric
- calm and intentional
- modern without looking sterile
- legible in product UI, login screens, decks, and browser tabs

Avoid:
- generic geometric SaaS logos
- AI sparkles, orbit icons, robot heads, or assistant clichés
- aggressive futurism
- playful cartoon energy

## Typography Direction
The wordmark should use a custom or custom-feeling type treatment with subtle irregularity. It should feel authored and human, not mathematically perfect.

Desired qualities:
- soft but not weak
- imperfect but not messy
- distinctive but restrained
- serious enough for enterprise software

Inspiration direction:
- imperfect grotesk
- softened neo-grotesk
- hand-tuned sans-serif
- subtle chalk or marker influence without looking childish

The `a` and `m` letterforms should carry the most personality.

Recommended UI/supporting fonts:
- Inter
- IBM Plex Sans
- Manrope

The wordmark should remain more distinctive than the supporting UI typography.

## Color System
The brand should be monochrome-first.

Core rule:
- dark background -> white `samm`
- light background -> black `samm`

Primary colors:
- Ink: `#0B0B0C`
- Paper: `#F5F3EF`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Black: `#000000`

Optional neutrals:
- Slate: `#6E6A63`
- Mist: `#DDD8D0`

Optional single accent, only if needed:
- Cobalt: `#3F6AE0`
or
- Moss: `#69725F`

Do not build the identity around multiple bright accents. Restraint is part of the brand.

## Tone of Voice
The voice should be:
- calm
- clear
- precise
- understated
- operational
- quietly confident

The product should not sound hype-heavy or theatrical.

Prefer:
- “Samm coordinates the flow.”
- “Review, approve, and route work.”
- “Specialist agents execute. Samm keeps them aligned.”

Avoid:
- “revolutionary AI magic”
- “superhuman productivity”
- “your friendly AI companion”

## Taglines
Preferred options:
- `orchestrate the work`
- `where agents align`
- `the system behind the system`

Secondary options:
- `coordination, clarified`
- `intelligence in flow`

## Product Expression
The dashboard and product surfaces should reflect the brand:
- minimal layout
- strong spacing
- clear hierarchy
- high legibility
- restrained color use
- no ornamental futurism

The interface should feel like an operating surface, not a marketing landing page.

## Mascot / Persona
`samm` is the coordinator identity, but it does not need a cartoon mascot. If personified at all, it should be done through typographic personality and restrained product language before any character illustration is introduced.

If a visual persona is explored later, it should be:
- abstract
- composed
- supervisory
- minimal

Not:
- cute
- overly human
- animated in a gimmicky way

## Usage Rules
- always present the brand as `samm`, not `SAMM`
- use the wordmark as the primary identifier
- keep branding high-contrast
- avoid decorative effects, gradients, glows, or unnecessary outlines
- preserve simplicity across dark and light backgrounds

Example 3:

## Brand Summary
`samm` is a quiet orchestration brand. It should feel human-guided, precise, and adaptable. The identity is not built on flashy AI symbolism. It is built on restraint, confidence, and a wordmark that feels slightly imperfect in exactly the right way.
