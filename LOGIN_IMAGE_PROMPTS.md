# samm Login Image Prompts

## Purpose
These prompts generate the four images for the `samm` login-screen 2x2 grid. The images should feel like one coherent set and depict the right users: founders, operators, small business owners, and lean teams.

## Shared Direction
Apply these qualities to all four images:
- modern business realism
- calm, capable people
- grounded environments
- lightly cinematic but not stylized
- natural light or soft controlled light
- no exaggerated smiling
- no cliché corporate stock poses
- no luxury excess
- no chaotic stress imagery
- no overprocessed AI-art look

Visual consistency targets:
- similar color temperature across all images
- realistic offices, desks, laptops, phones, notebooks
- believable business settings
- subtle sense of focus and relief
- suitable for crop use in a dashboard login panel

## Prompt 1: Founder Reviewing Performance
```text
Create a realistic editorial-style image of a small business founder reviewing marketing or business performance on a laptop in a calm workspace. The person should look focused, capable, and slightly relieved rather than stressed. The environment should feel modern and practical, not luxurious, with natural light, a clean desk, and believable business context. The mood should communicate control returning after chaos. Avoid stock-photo smiles, fake corporate posing, and overly polished ad aesthetics.
```

## Prompt 2: Lean Team Planning Together
```text
Create a realistic image of a small, lean business team in a calm planning session around a table or shared workspace. They should look engaged, practical, and aligned, not overly animated or staged. Include laptops, notebooks, or screens in a believable way. The scene should feel like a real working team coordinating marketing and operations, with modern but grounded styling. Avoid boardroom clichés, handshakes, exaggerated laughter, and generic stock-photo energy.
```

## Prompt 3: Operator Monitoring From A Dashboard
```text
Create a realistic image of a business operator or coordinator working from a laptop or desktop dashboard, monitoring activity and making decisions calmly. The person should appear composed and in control, with visible screen glow or interface context but no flashy sci-fi styling. The setting should feel practical and professional, with subtle depth and a focused atmosphere. The image should suggest operational clarity, approval workflows, and coordinated execution. Avoid hacker aesthetics, dark cyberpunk lighting, and overdramatic tech visuals.
```

## Prompt 4: Owner In A Composed Work Moment
```text
Create a realistic image of a small business owner or manager in a composed work moment, using a phone and laptop or reviewing notes while staying calm and focused. The person should feel responsible and experienced, not overwhelmed. The environment should be believable and modestly professional, with soft light and a real sense of daily business rhythm. The image should imply that work is moving under control rather than falling apart. Avoid luxury branding, call-center visuals, and generic smiling stock photography.
```

## Set Consistency Prompt
Use this if your image tool supports a master instruction across all four generations.

```text
Generate a cohesive 4-image set for a software login screen. The subjects should be founders, operators, small business owners, and lean teams in realistic business environments. The visual tone should be calm, grounded, modern, and lightly cinematic. Avoid stock-photo clichés, exaggerated expressions, over-stylization, and luxury office aesthetics. The set should feel coherent enough to appear as a 2x2 grid on a software login screen.
```

## Optional Regional Fit Add-On
Use if you want stronger African and emerging-market resonance without making the images feel narrowly regional.

```text
Cast the subjects and environments in a way that feels globally relevant and comfortably aligned with African and emerging-market business contexts: practical spaces, modern devices, real working professionals, no exoticizing, no stereotypes.
```

## Optional Cropping Add-On
Use if the tool tends to produce awkward framing.

```text
Compose the image so it remains strong when cropped into a rectangular panel inside a 2x2 grid. Keep the subject clear and the environment readable without relying on wide panoramic detail.
```

## Reject If The Output Feels Like
- generic stock business photography
- overly corporate boardroom imagery
- startup hype content
- chaotic crisis scenes
- luxury lifestyle advertising
- glossy AI-generated perfection with no realism

## Selection Criteria
Choose the set that best communicates:
- calm capability
- operational relevance
- business ownership and responsibility
- relief from marketing chaos
- coherence across all four panels
