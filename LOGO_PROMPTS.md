# samm Logo Prompt Set

## Purpose
This prompt set is for generating the `samm` wordmark only. The goal is to explore high-quality directions for a lowercase, slightly imperfect brand identity that feels human, intelligent, and calm.

These prompts should not produce icons, mascots, symbols, or generic AI graphics. The focus is the wordmark itself.

## Non-Negotiables
- use the word `samm` in lowercase only
- wordmark only, no standalone symbol
- black on white or white on black
- flat vector look
- no gradients, glows, 3D, chrome, or mockup effects
- no robot, orbit, sparkle, or chatbot imagery
- avoid perfect geometric sans styling
- avoid childish handwritten or comic styling
- the result should feel authored, not accidental

## Core Prompt
```text
Design a lowercase wordmark logo for “samm” for an AI orchestration software brand. The typography should feel slightly imperfect, human, calm, and intelligent, with subtle irregularities in the letterforms. Avoid perfect geometry. The wordmark should feel serious enough for business software, but distinct from generic enterprise SaaS branding. Use only the word “samm” with no symbol or icon. Black on white background. Minimal flat vector style. No gradients, no 3D, no decorative effects.
```

## Prompt Add-Ons
Use these add-ons selectively if a generation tool needs stronger constraints.

Small-size legibility:
```text
Ensure the wordmark remains legible and recognizable at favicon-adjacent sizes and in narrow product headers.
```

Avoid over-stylization:
```text
Do not make the logo look handwritten, childish, retro, graffiti-like, or fashion-editorial to the point of losing software credibility.
```

More warmth:
```text
Introduce a touch more softness and humanity in the curves while preserving clarity and trust.
```

More restraint:
```text
Reduce expressiveness slightly and make the logo feel more operational and composed.
```

## Evaluation Criteria
When reviewing outputs, prefer options that:
- feel custom without looking forced
- read clearly as `samm`
- work equally on dark and light backgrounds
- avoid startup AI clichés
- feel calm rather than flashy
- balance originality with business trust

Reject options that:
- feel childish
- look like a generic geometric SaaS font
- become too futuristic or too decorative
- depend on effects instead of typography
- look better as art than as a usable software wordmark
